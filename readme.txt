== Description ==

Click Dynamics is a unique way of monetizing your content, we turn words into money!

Click Dynamics was created to offer something completely unique to the online advertising market. Its is a product that offers first and foremost the ability to automatically place geo-targeted, contextual text links to editorial content. With this level of focus we are able to bring users highly relevant ads based on the niche they are reading about.

**How Does It Work?**
Our system scans your page for the highest-paying keywords. The keywords for each page on your website are then indexed by our systems and hyperlinks are automatically created and turns them into ads. No linking or work required by you, our system does it for you!

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

e.g.

1. Upload the `clickdynamics` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. On the left-hand side menu, click on 'Click Dynamics'
4. Register for Click Dynamics on the www.clickdynamics.com website if you haven't done already.
5. Once your site is approved, add the UID and ad code.
